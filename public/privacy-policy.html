Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how TravelPass Ireland Ltd collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our digital toll pass service.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number
  • Vehicle Information: Registration number, make, model, year
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details (processed securely)
  • Identity Verification: Government ID for account verification

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns
  • Location Data: General location for service optimization
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy for details

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Process and activate your travel passes
  • Verify your identity and vehicle details
  • Process payments and issue receipts
  • Provide customer support

Communication

  • Send order confirmations and updates
  • Notify you of service changes
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send promotional offers (with consent)

Business Operations

  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

Service Providers

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal for secure payment processing
  • Email Services: For sending confirmations and communications
  • Cloud Storage: AWS, Google Cloud for secure data storage
  • Analytics: Google Analytics for usage insights

Legal Requirements

  • Government agencies when required by law
  • Law enforcement for fraud prevention
  • Courts when subject to legal proceedings
  • Regulatory bodies for compliance purposes

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Transaction Records: 7 years (for tax and legal purposes)
  • Marketing Data: Until you unsubscribe
  • Support Communications: 3 years from last contact

Your Rights

Under GDPR and Irish data protection law, you have the right to:

Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • Transfer your data to another service

Correction and Deletion

  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict processing of your data

Consent Management

  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Object to data processing
  • Opt out of automated decision-making

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in encrypted, access-controlled systems
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access on need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Incident Response: Procedures for data breach notification

International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside the EU. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses with service providers
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
  • Privacy Shield framework compliance

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notification to registered users
  • Prominent notice on our website
  • Updated "Last modified" date
